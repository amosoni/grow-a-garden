üìù Step-by-Step Wheat Growing Guide

Passo 1: Prepare Your Farm

Set up your wheat farming area:

  • Clear the land of weeds and rocks
  • Ensure good soil quality
  • Create efficient watering system
  • Plan for easy harvesting access

Passo 2: Obtain Wheat Seeds

Get your wheat seeds ready:

  • Purchase Seeds: Buy from seed shop or market
  • Check Quality: Ensure seeds are fresh and viable
  • Plan Quantity: Calculate how many seeds you need
  • Store Properly: Keep seeds in dry, cool place

Passo 3: Plant Wheat Seeds

Plant your wheat seeds correctly:

  • Dig small holes in prepared soil
  • Place seeds at proper depth (1-2 inches)
  • Cover seeds with soil
  • Space plants appropriately (6-8 inches apart)
  • Water immediately after planting

Passo 4: Water and Care

Maintain your wheat crop:

  • Water regularly (every 15-20 min)
  • Water during golden hours for bonus growth
  • Remove any weeds that appear
  • Monitor for pests or diseases
  • Ensure adequate sunlight exposure

Passo 5: Monitor Growth

Track your wheat's development:

  • Watch for sprouting (first few minutes)
  • Monitor stem growth (15-30 min)
  • Check for grain formation (30-45 min)
  • Look for golden color indicating maturity
  • Time your harvest perfectly

Passo 6: Harvest Wheat

Harvest your wheat at the right time:

  • Wait for full maturity (45 min total)
  • Look for golden-brown color
  • Use appropriate harvesting tools
  • Collect all wheat grains
  • Store harvested wheat properly

‚ùì Perguntas frequentes

Q: How long does it take for wheat to grow?

A: Wheat takes exactly 45 min to reach full maturity from planting to harvest.

Q: How much wheat can I harvest from one plant?

A: Each wheat plant typically yields 1-2 wheat grains, depending on growing conditions and care.

Q: What's the best time to plant wheat?

A: Plant wheat during golden hours (7:00-9:00 AM) for the fastest growth and best yields.

Q: How much profit can I make from wheat farming?

A: Wheat typically sells for 50-100 moedas per grain. With efficient farming, you can earn 500-1000 moedas per harvest cycle.

Q: Should I sell wheat raw or process it into flour?

A: Process wheat into flour for 3-4x the profit. Flour sells for much more than raw wheat.

Q: How do I prevent wheat from dying?

A: Water regularly, avoid overwatering, plant in good soil, and harvest on time. Don't let wheat sit too long after maturity.
