🎯 Why Grow Oranges?

  • High Value: Premium fruit used in smoothies and juices
  • Year-Round Demand: Consistent market interest
  • Scalable Orchards: Works well with modular farm design

🥘 Requirements

🌱 Saplings

Buy from sapling shop

💧 Water

Deep watering every cycle

🌞 Full Sun

Open areas with strong sunlight

✂️ Pruning

Seasonal pruning to improve airflow

🛠️ Tools

Watering can, pruning shears

📝 Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Orchard

  • Plan 3x3 or 4x4 spacing for trees
  • Ensure easy watering access

Step 2: Plant Saplings

  • Dig holes and plant upright
  • Backfill and water thoroughly

Step 3: Water & Prune

  • Water every 15–20 minutes in-game
  • Prune inner branches for airflow and fruit size

Step 4: Fruit & Harvest

  • Harvest at full orange color
  • Store cool; sell fresh or use in recipes

💡 Pro Tips

Golden Hours: Water 07:00–09:00 to boost growth.
Thinning: Remove excess small fruits to increase size.
Recipe Synergy: Reserve best oranges for premium smoothies/juices.
