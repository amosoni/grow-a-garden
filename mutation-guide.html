üß¨ Mutation Basics

The mutation system is one of the most complex and rewarding features in Grow a Garden. Understanding how mutations work is crucial for creating valuable crops and maximizing profits.

What are Mutations?

Mutations are special variations of crops that occur when specific conditions are met during the growing process. These mutations can have enhanced properties such as increased yield, faster growth, higher value, or unique visual characteristics.

Core Mutation Principles:

  1. Compatibility: Not all crops can be combined for mutations
  2. Conditions: Specific growing conditions increase mutation chances
  3. Rarity: Mutations have different rarity levels affecting their value
  4. Inheritance: Some mutations can be passed down to offspring
  5. Stability: Mutations may be stable or unstable
üí° Pro Tip: Start with basic mutations before attempting complex combinations. Patience and experimentation are key to success!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How do I start with mutations?

A: Begin with basic crop combinations and gradually experiment with more complex combinations. Focus on learning the system before attempting rare mutations.

Q: What increases mutation chances?

A: Perfect growing conditions, compatible crop families, mutation boosters, golden hours, and special events all increase mutation chances.

Q: Are mutations permanent?

A: Some mutations are stable and can be reproduced, while others are unstable and may not occur again. Documentation helps identify patterns.

Advanced Questions

Q: Can I sell mutations?

A: Yes, mutations often have significantly higher market value than regular crops. Rare mutations can be extremely valuable.

Q: How do I create legendary mutations?

A: Legendary mutations require perfect conditions, rare ingredients, and often special events. They're extremely rare and valuable.

Q: Do mutations affect crafting?

A: Yes, mutated crops often unlock special crafting recipes and can create enhanced versions of items.
