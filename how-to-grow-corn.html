🎯 Why Grow Corn?

  • High Yield: Consistent production per field
  • Recipe Demand: Used for advanced cooking and animal feed
  • Stable Profit: Good ROI with proper timing

🥘 Requirements

🌽 Corn Seeds

Buy from seed shop

💧 Water

Regular deep watering

🌞 Sunlight

Full sun exposure

🧪 Fertilizer

Apply at knee-high stage

🛠️ Tools

Watering can, hoe

📝 Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Field

  • Clear weeds and set straight rows (lanes every 2–3 rows)
  • Improve soil with compost

Step 2: Plant Seeds

  • Depth: 1–2 inches; Spacing: 8–12 inches
  • Water immediately after planting

Step 3: Water & Fertilize

  • Deep watering every cycle; golden-hour loops 07:00–09:00
  • Fertilize when plants reach knee height

Step 4: Harvest

  • Harvest when ears are full and silks are brown/dry
  • Store cool; sell in bundles

💡 Pro Tips

Block Planting: Plant in squares rather than single rows for better pollination.
Water Discipline: Deep, less frequent watering improves root strength.
Market Timing: Bundle ears and sell at peak hours for higher prices.
