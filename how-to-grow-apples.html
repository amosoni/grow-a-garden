🎯 Apple Growing Overview

Apples are a premium fruit used in profitable recipes like pies and smoothies. Proper pruning and watering significantly improve yields.

  • High Value: Used in pies and premium juices
  • Orchard Scaling: Plant multiple saplings for steady supply
  • Market Demand: Consistently high among players

🥘 Growing Requirements

🌱 Apple Saplings

Buy from seed/sapling shop

💧 Water

Deep, regular watering

🌞 Full Sun

Open areas with strong sunlight

✂️ Pruning

Prune for airflow and fruit size

⏰ Time

~60 minutes to mature

🛠️ Tools

Watering can, pruning shears

📝 Step-by-Step Apple Growing Guide

Step 1: Prepare Orchard

  • Choose a flat, sunny area
  • Plan 3x3 or 4x4 spacing for trees
  • Ensure easy watering access

Step 2: Plant Saplings

  • Dig holes and place saplings upright
  • Backfill and firm soil gently
  • Water thoroughly after planting

Step 3: Water & Prune

  • Water every 15-20 minutes in-game
  • Prune inner branches for airflow
  • Use golden hours (7:00-9:00) for growth boost

Step 4: Fruit Set & Harvest

  • Watch for color change to deep red
  • Harvest gently to avoid damage
  • Store in cool containers before selling

💡 Pro Tips

Prune Smart: Remove crossing branches to improve fruit size.
Golden Hours: Watering during 7:00-9:00 boosts growth speed.
Batch Harvest: Stagger planting for continuous fruit supply.
Recipe Synergy: Reserve premium apples for pies and smoothies.
