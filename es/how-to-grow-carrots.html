Carrots are a staple crop used in various recipes. They grow relatively quickly and offer consistent profits when farmed correctly.

  • Steady Demand: Used in cooking and trade
  • Quick Cycle: Short growth time with proper care
  • Low Cost: Cheap seeds and simple care routine
  • Scalable: Works well for batch farming

üìù Step-by-Step Carrot Growing Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Farm

  • Clear rocks and loosen soil to a fine texture
  • Plan rows for easy watering and harvesting
  • Ensure full-sun exposure

Step 2: Plant Seeds

  • Plant seeds 1 inch deep with 3-4 inches spacing
  • Cover lightly and water immediately

Step 3: Water & Care

  • Water lightly every 10-15 minutes in-game
  • Use golden hours (7:00-9:00) for bonus growth
  • Keep soil moist; avoid waterlogging

Step 4: Thin Seedlings

  • Thin crowded areas for thicker, straighter roots

Step 5: Harvest

  • Harvest when tops are mature and roots are full-sized
  • Pull gently to avoid breakage
