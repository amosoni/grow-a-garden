🎯 Why Grow Berries?

  • High Profit: Used in smoothies, pies, and premium recipes
  • Fast Cycle: Shorter time to first harvest
  • Scalable: Great for modular farms

🥘 Requirements

🪴 Seedlings

Strawberry, Blueberry, Raspberry

💧 Water

Frequent light watering

🌞 Sun

Full sun for best sweetness

✂️ Pruning

Remove old canes after harvest

🛠️ Tools

Shears, watering can

📝 Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Beds

  • Raised beds with rich compost
  • Mulch to retain moisture

Step 2: Plant & Space

  • 12–16 inch spacing
  • Water immediately

Step 3: Water & Prune

  • Light daily watering
  • Remove old flowering canes

Step 4: Harvest & Sell

  • Pick fully colored berries
  • Sell in bundles for premium price

💡 Pro Tips

Golden Hours: Morning watering increases sweetness and yield.
Crop Rotation: Rotate beds yearly to avoid diseases.
Recipe Synergy: Reserve best berries for smoothies and pies.
