📝 Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Pasta Dough

Mix flour and eggs to create pasta dough

Knead until smooth and elastic

Step 2: Make Pasta

Roll out dough and cut into spaghetti strands

Allow pasta to dry slightly

Step 3: Prepare Sauce

Cook tomatoes with herbs and spices

Simmer until thickened

Step 4: Cook and Serve

Boil pasta in salted water

Combine with sauce and serve hot

💡 Pro Tips

Perfect Dough

Use fresh eggs and high-quality flour for best results.

Cooking Time

Cook pasta al dente for perfect texture.

Sauce Consistency

Let sauce simmer to develop rich flavors.
