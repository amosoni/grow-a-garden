📦 Storage and Logistics in Grow a Garden

Optimization Guide: Storage Hubs, Labeling, Routing, and Delivery

  1. 🏠 Home
  2. 📚 Guides
  3. 📦 Storage & Logistics

🎯 Goals

  • Reduce walking distance and idle time
  • Keep inventory clear and easy to find
  • Deliver goods in efficient batches

🏗️ Storage Hub Design

📍 Central Placement

Between fields and processing line.

🏷️ Labeling

One chest per ingredient; distinct icons/colors.

🧭 Routing

Clockwise pickup and delivery loops.

🚪 Access

2-tile paths; avoid choke points.

🧊 Cold Storage

Separate finished goods to avoid mixing.

🧮 Buffer Stock

Keep small buffer to prevent line stops.

🚚 Delivery & Market Strategy

Batching

  • Deliver full stacks to reduce trips
  • Bundle related goods (e.g., pies + smoothies)

Timing

  • Sell during peak hours
  • Align with co-op selling windows

Routing

  • Plan loop: storage → market → storage
  • Drop-off near entrance for quick turnarounds
