🍝 如何在Grow a Garden中制作意大利面

完整指南：从配料收集到美味上桌

📋 所需配料

  • 🌾 Wheat Flour - 2 cups
  • 🥚 Eggs - 2 pieces
  • 🍅 Tomatoes - 3 pieces
  • 🧄 Garlic - 2 cloves
  • 🌿 Basil - 1 bunch
  • 🧂 Salt - to taste

👨‍🍳 制作步骤

  1. Mix flour and eggs to make pasta dough
  2. Roll out the dough and cut into strips
  3. Boil the pasta in salted water
  4. Prepare tomato sauce with garlic and basil
  5. Combine pasta with sauce and serve

💡 制作技巧

  • Use fresh ingredients for better taste
  • Don't overcook the pasta
  • Save some pasta water for sauce consistency
  • Add cheese on top for extra flavor
