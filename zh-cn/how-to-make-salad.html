🌱 基础沙拉制作

在种植花园中， salad making is one of the core gameplay mechanics. By growing various vegetables and fruits, you can create different levels of salads to earn rewards.

制作步骤：

  1. 收集食材: Plant basic vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, carrots in your garden
  2. 浇水管理: Regularly water your plants to ensure healthy growth
  3. 收获时机: Wait for plants to fully mature before harvesting
  4. 制作沙拉: Put collected ingredients into the salad maker
  5. 获得奖励: Complete salad making to earn game currency and experience
💡 贴士： Watering during golden hours (7:00-9:00 in-game) provides double effects, greatly increasing plant growth speed!

💡 Making Tips & Tricks

Time Management Tips

  • Staggered Planting: Different plants have different growth times, arrange planting order reasonably
  • Batch Making: Collect enough ingredients then make multiple salads at once
  • Golden Hours: Water during 7:00-9:00 in-game for double effects
  • Daily Login: Maintain daily login for extra rewards

效率提升方法

  • Priority Planting: Plant short-growth-time plants first for quick basic ingredients
  • Upgrade Tools: Use better watering cans and planting tools
  • Friend Cooperation: Exchange ingredients with friends to complement shortages
  • Event Participation: Participate in game events for rare ingredients
🎯 贴士： When making salads, ingredient freshness affects final quality. Try to use freshly harvested ingredients for higher rewards!
⚠️ Important Note: Some rare ingredients only appear during specific times or events. Missing opportunities require waiting for the next event. Plan your planting schedule in advance!

❓ 常见问题

Q: How to quickly earn more coins?

A: Making advanced salads is the best way to earn coins. We recommend planting high-value ingredients like avocados and strawberries to make luxury salad recipes.

Q: Why are my plants growing slowly?

A: Ensure regular watering, especially during golden hours (7:00-9:00) for best effects. Using better watering tools can also improve efficiency.

Q: Where to get rare ingredients?

A: Rare ingredients are mainly obtained through game events, special events, or advanced seed packs. Follow game announcements and participate in events promptly.

Q: How to make the highest level salads?

A: Need to collect various rare ingredients like mushrooms, chili peppers, mangoes, etc. We recommend first establishing stable basic ingredient supply, then gradually collecting rare ingredients.

Q: What if salad making fails?

A: Check if ingredients are fresh enough, ensure recipe is correct. Some advanced recipes require specific amounts of ingredients, please verify carefully.

Q: How to cooperate with other players?

A: Join Discord communities to exchange experience with other players and trade ingredients. Team cooperation can greatly improve efficiency.
