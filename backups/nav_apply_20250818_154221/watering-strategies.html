‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is the best time to water crops?

A: Water during golden hours (7:00-9:00 in-game time) for double growth effects and maximum efficiency.

Q: How often should I water my crops?

A: Watering frequency depends on crop type - leafy greens every 2-3 hours, root vegetables every 3-4 hours, fruit trees every 4-6 hours.

Q: What's the most efficient watering method?

A: Drip irrigation is the most efficient method, followed by automated watering systems.

Q: How do I know if my crops need water?

A: Check soil moisture visually, feel the soil, or watch for plant signs like wilting leaves.

Q: Can I overwater my crops?

A: Yes, overwatering can harm crops. Monitor soil moisture and avoid watering when soil is already wet.

Q: How can I improve my watering efficiency?

A: Use golden hours, invest in better tools, automate your system, and monitor crop response to optimize your watering strategy.
