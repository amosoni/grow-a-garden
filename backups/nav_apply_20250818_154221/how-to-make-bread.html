üçû Basic Bread Making

In Grow a Garden, bread making is one of the fundamental crafting activities. By growing wheat and other ingredients, you can create various types of bread to earn rewards and progress in the game.

Making Steps:

  1. Grow Wheat: Plant wheat seeds and water regularly for healthy growth
  2. Harvest Wheat: Wait for wheat to fully mature before harvesting
  3. Grind Flour: Process harvested wheat into flour
  4. Mix Dough: Combine flour, water, yeast, and salt
  5. Bake Bread: Put dough into the bread maker to create bread
üí° Pro Tip: Watering during golden hours (7:00-9:00 in-game) provides double effects, greatly increasing wheat growth speed and flour quality!
