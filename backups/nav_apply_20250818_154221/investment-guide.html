‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How much should I invest?

A: Start with small amounts (10-20% of your earnings) and gradually increase as you gain experience and confidence.

Q: What's the best investment strategy?

A: The best strategy depends on your goals and risk tolerance. Start with a balanced approach and adjust based on results.

Q: How long should I hold investments?

A: Hold investments until they reach your target returns or market conditions change significantly.

Advanced Questions

Q: How do I maximize returns?

A: Diversify your portfolio, research thoroughly, time your investments well, and reinvest profits for compound growth.

Q: What's the risk of losing money?

A: All investments carry some risk. Manage risk through diversification, proper research, and never investing more than you can afford to lose.

Q: How do I track my investments?

A: Keep detailed records of all investments, including purchase price, sale price, timing, and reasons for decisions.
