‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How often do events occur?

A: Events typically occur weekly, with major events happening monthly. Special events may be announced with varying frequency.

Q: Do I need to participate in all events?

A: No, focus on events that align with your goals and interests. Quality participation is better than spreading yourself thin.

Q: Can I get event rewards later?

A: Most event rewards are exclusive to the event period. Some may return in future events, but this is not guaranteed.

Q: How do I maximize event rewards?

A: Prepare in advance, participate consistently, focus on specific goals, and collaborate with the community when beneficial.

Q: Are events worth the time investment?

A: Events often provide unique rewards and accelerated progression that can significantly benefit your long-term gameplay.

Q: How do I prepare for upcoming events?

A: Monitor event announcements, stockpile relevant resources, upgrade necessary tools, and coordinate with your community.
